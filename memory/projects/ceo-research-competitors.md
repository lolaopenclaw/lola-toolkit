## Executive Summary

European cloud providers are aggressively investing in AI infrastructure and sovereign cloud solutions, driven by geopolitical tensions and data sovereignty concerns. **European sovereign cloud spending is projected to grow 83% in 2026 to $12.6B** (Gartner, Feb 2026), nearly doubling again to $23B by 2027.

**Key Finding:** Competitors are restructuring around AI-first product organizations, with specialized AI Product Management roles emerging as critical differentiators. The transition from traditional Product Owners to evolved AI Product Managers is already underway across the market.
